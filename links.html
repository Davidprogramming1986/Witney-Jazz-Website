WitneyJazz

We’re keen to let you know about all kinds of great musicians, organisations and events that we know of - here are some of them for you to click on:

Paul Jefferies - Live Music for events / Music Lessons

Paul is professional musician. He teaches double bass (classical and jazz, bass guitar and guitar. Beginners especially welcome. Paul also arranges and books live music for private, corporate and commercial events.

Ben Holder - Jazz Violinist

Ben is one of Europes finest jazz swing violinists, he’s also a composer, magician and all round top musician.

Rob Terry Trio

A superb piano, bass and drums trio that excels at performances that combine classical and jazz music influences. Their music can be downloaded or purchase direct via this website which also lists their concert details.

Moreton-in-Marsh Jazz

Also known as Jazz at The Redesdale, this club is run by Ben Holder and Paul Jefferies and provides a monthly diet of intimate performances from some of the nations finest musicians. The music played is inclined towards the swing side of the genre. Contact the guys via this link or by emailing steppelli@yahoo.co.uk

Simon Dubber - violins, cellos (sales and repairs)

Simon repairs and supplies violins, cellos and double basses as well as a range of accessories.

Peter Crowther - pianos and Cellos (sales, hire and repair)

Peter repairs and supplies violins, cellos and double basses as well as a range of accessories. he also hires out pianos.

Select CDs (online shop for great music CDs)

Select CDs started very much as a passion project, with the aim of making the musical releases of the UK's finest musicians available to as many people as possible.

Rapture

Open 7 days a week, these guys offer a huge range of CDs, DVDs and now a handsome collection of used and new vinyl records. There’s also delicious fresh artisan coffee served in the vinyl department at their Mostro cafe.

Witney Music Festival

An excellent, free music festival which is held annually around Witney and features a host of music styles, local and nationally acclaimed bands and a fistful of household names.
